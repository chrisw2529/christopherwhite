H2Grow

H2Grow is an android app that sends the user a notification when it is time to water their household plants. The app can also suggest watering intervals for a plant, it does this by using an SQL database that holds plant information for most of the common household plants.

The app is very simple to use. start by adding a plant. All of the users’ plant information is stored in an SQL database.


The app comes with an auto-fill feature of plants that are also stored in an SQL database.


Once plants are added they show up on the main screen, letting the user know how long until they need to be watered. The leaf on the side changes color from green to yellow to red indicating the amount of time until the plant needs to be watered. A notification will be sent to your phone when the plant needs water.

Once you water the plant, click on it and select ‘water’. The timer indicating when to water the plant, will reset.

You can also simply swipe right on the main screen to tell the app that your plant has been watered.

The order in which your plants appear can be sorted alphabetically or according to next watering time.

Notifications can be turned on or off through the app. You also have the option to integrate your watering schedule into your phone's calendar.
