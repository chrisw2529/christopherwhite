H2Grow

H2Grow is an android app that remindes you when its time to water your plants.

Start by adding a plant. All of the users plant information is stored in a SQL database.

Once plants are added they show up on the main screen it lets you know how long until they need to be watered. The leaf on the side changes color from green to yellow to red when the plant gets closer to needing to be watered. A notification will be sent to your phone when the plant needs water.

Once you water it just click on the plant and click ‘water’ and the timer resets.

You can also simply swipe right on the main screen to tell the app that your plant has been watered.

The order in which your plants appear can be sorted according to next watering time or alphabetically.

Notifications can be turned on or off through the app. You also have the option to integrate your watering schedule into your phone's calendar.
