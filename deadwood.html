Deadwood

This project is a replica of the boardgame Deadwood by cheapass games. The game board was made in Java swing and all of the background logic was programmed in Java.

The goal of deadwood is to score the most amount of points at the end of the game. The players are depicted as dice and the number shown on the die is the players current rank. Players can rank up by spending money or fame tokens which are acquired by taking ‘acting roles’ and successfully acting on them. At the end of the game a player's total points is calculated by a combination of rank, money, and fame tokens that they acquired throughout the game, the player with the most wins. This game supports 2 to 3 players.

This was made with a partner as a class project.
